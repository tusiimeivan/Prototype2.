About Agric-Bridge International

At Agric-Bridge International, we see a world where the farmer who grows your food is not a stranger, but a partner. We are a purpose-driven technology company founded on a simple but powerful belief: the distance between a local farmer and the global market should be a bridge, not a barrier.
Our platform dismantles the traditional, inefficient supply chains that often leave smallholder farmers underpaid and international buyers disconnected from their source. We replace them with a transparent, direct, and fair digital marketplace. By leveraging technology, we are not just facilitating trade; we are fostering a global community built on mutual respect, fair economics, and a shared commitment to quality and sustainability. AgriBridge Global is more than a platform it's a movement towards a more equitable and connected global food system.


Mission of the Company

Our mission is to empower local farmers by providing direct access to the international market, fostering sustainable economic growth, and building a transparent future for global agriculture.

How to buy

  1. First sign up for a buyer's account
  2. Verify your identity as a buyer using your national Id
  3. A buyer's identity number will be sent to you via email
  4. Select the agricultural products to purchase and quantity.Prices are displayed on each agricultural product
  5. Select means of payment and destination
  6. Order and make payments for the agricultural products you have selected
  7. Confirmation of order and duration of delivery will be sent via email

How to sell

  1. First sign up for a seller's account
  2. Verify your identity as a seller using your national Id
  3. A seller's identity number will be sent to you via email
  4. Select the agricultural products to sell and quantity. Prices are displayed on each agricultural product
  5. Select means of payment
  6. Deliver the agricultural products to sell at the company's ware house.Location will be sent via email
  7. Present the products to the company's management for inspection
  8. After inspection, the farmer is paid according to the selected means of payment
